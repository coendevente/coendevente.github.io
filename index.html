Research projects

Estimating Uncertainty of Deep Neural Networks for Age-related Macular Degeneration Grading using OCT

We present a method for providing confidence scores of CNNs for age-related macular degeneration (AMD) grading in optical coherence tomography (OCT).

Automated COVID-19 Grading in CT Scans

Amidst the ongoing pandemic, several studies have shown that COVID-19 classification and grading using CT images can be automated with CNNs. We systematically investigated the effectiveness of several technical aspects for this task.

Prostate Cancer Detection and Grading in bpMRI

Neural networks that simultaneously detect and grade cancer tissue in an end-to-end fashion.
